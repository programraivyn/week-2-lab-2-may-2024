Cheers to all the Self-Proclaimed Chefs!

I'll bet you might be wondering..

What made me get into cooking? Well, that's a great question! I'd always had the passion for cooking, especially experimentally since I was
a little girl. I'd watch Food Network faithfully, and because of our convenient set-up, I was able to practice a lot of dishes on my own while watching
many of my favorites like Emeril, Rachel Ray, Barefoot Contessa, Bobby Flay, and more! Although I honed my cooking skills from the time of adolescence,
it wasn't until COVID-19 struck that I really began exploring with culinary arts. Being restricted to the house opened me up to a world of new recipes and cooking
ideas that have stuck with me to this day. I also have more of an appreciation for homemade meals now that the majority of my food is prepared at home.

Now that you've read up on my journey into the cooking world, allow me to share my Top 5 Favorite Dishes of All Time, and foods I'm interested in cooking soon!

  • Balsamic Glazed Lamb Chops
  • Stuffed Bell Peppers
  • Seared Bass
